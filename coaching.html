Coaching

At Victory Fitness, we offer various types of coaching to suit our clients' needs: Premium Coaching, Semi-Private Coaching, and Online Coaching.

Premium Coaching

Premium Coaching is a private personal training experience that gives you 100% dedicated time with your personal trainer.

You receive the privacy of a fully-equipped training studio. This appeals to two types of clients:

  1. Clients who lack confidence and may feel anxious in a commercial gym environment without supervision from a personal trainer
  2. Sports-specific clients who require our full attention to work on a set goal.

You will receive consistent support to your training, your nutrition and mindset. If you feel that you have been going around in circles and you feel you have got nowhere then a structured coaching plan is recommended for you.

In a private studio session with your coach, you will go through strategic planning, creating a roadmap, addressing any issues, and overcoming any battles you may come up against during your fitness journey.

Here is a list of what you will gain in your premium plan:

  • Nutritional examination and structured plan, developed from a lifestyle examination
  • One-to-one coaching in the studio (structured times throughout the week)
  • Progress reviews
  • Support and guidance every step of the way
  • Access to your workouts through a guided app

Semi-Private Coaching

Semi-Private is an exciting new way to train as the training involves a small group of people. The group environment will inspire you to keep going when it gets tough and reduces the per-person cost which makes it more cost effective.

You are coached through your program in a small group of like-minded people with everyone having a programme tailored specifically to them. It is a beautiful mix of personal coaching & community that develops both autonomy and a sense of togetherness.

You will gain a bespoke programme based on your goals and fitness level. Through camaraderie with like-minded people, you will gain accountability and consistency and will stay on track with your fitness goals. As the groups are designed to be small, dedicated time with your coach will still be provided.

Through the group environment, you have access to several training sessions a week as opposed to one or two. This is a great way to improve techniques or expedite your goals.

Online Coaching

Our online personal coaching is ideal for individuals that want the greatest return from their training efforts but out of the comfort of their home.

Our guidance and knowledge will guarantee that you have an expert coach with you every step of the way, giving you consistent, guided results.

What you will receive:

  • A transformation roadmap — A Zoom call with your trainer to discuss your full fitness history and your future goals. Together, you will build a roadmap to visualise your path to success
  • A bespoke coaching program — A plan that ensures your goals will be hit, taking your lifestyle into account
  • Access to your own App — An app with personalized login, through which all workouts will be delivered
  • Consistent supervision — Weekly check-ins with your trainer to iron out any issues and make any adjustments to your plan

The results you can expect:

No matter what equipment you have available, your coach will accelerate your results with guidance and support. You will receive the tools to achieve the targets you have set for yourself.
